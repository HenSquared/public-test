# The Weight of a Moment

Time is a river that never returns,
carrying embers of all that we've learned.
The laughter of morning, the hush of the night,
each second a candle that flickers with light.

We reach for the hours that slip through our hands,
like grains of gold sand on far-distant lands.
Yet in every goodbye and every new start,
there lives a small moment that beats like a heart.

So hold what is fleeting, breathe in what is here,
the warmth of a friendship, a voice ringing clear.
For time may rush onward like wind through the trees,
but the moments we cherish stay with us, at ease.

---

*Written for the ages — and for today.*
